About Me

Hi, I'm Faisal!

I have started my PhD studies in computer science at the University of California, Riverside. Currently, I am on leave as a Lecturer at Brac University, Bangladesh. I graduated from Islamic University of Technology with a BSc in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). I completed Higher Secondary at Notre Dame College, and Secondary School at Chittagong Collegiate School. I'm from a very beautiful part of the country called Chattogram.

My research focuses the application of Machine Learning and Deep Learning in Bioinformatics, Computational Biology, and Data Science. My vision is to make the best use of data (numeric, image, texts) to build efficient models with the help of domain expert knowledge.
